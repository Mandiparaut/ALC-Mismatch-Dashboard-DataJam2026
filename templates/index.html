🔑 Key Finding: Social Vulnerability Drives ALC, Not Medical Need
Halifax research found that social vulnerability — not medical complexity — is the strongest predictor of ALC status. Patients are stuck in hospital because they lack home support networks, not because they require ongoing medical treatment.

This means the fix is a routing problem, not a capacity problem. The resources exist. They're just pointed at the wrong places.

The MRI formula applied: NS is using hospital beds (a resource) for ALC patients (the wrong purpose). The fix is targeting home care to the zones with the worst mismatch — no hiring, no building.
